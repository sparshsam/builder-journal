Your public builder journal and ecosystem command center. Tracks the direction and status of your software ecosystem at a public-safe level.

## Rules

3. ecosystem-standards is canonical. When this repo conflicts, ecosystem-standards wins.
4. Keep entries factual, restrained, and clear.
